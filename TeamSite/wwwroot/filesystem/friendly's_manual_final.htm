Fountain

F1.1  All items including allergens labeled, separated with no cross contamination

 

Threshold: Cite if one occurrence is observed.

 

 

Assessment:

Check that all ice cream flavors are labeled with a red label and separated from non-allergens as much as possible to prevent cross contamination (placed in back row or separate cabinet).  Allergen toppings are also required to have a red label and spoon and should be stored below or separate from non-allergen toppings. 

 

Check that M&M’s and Reese’s are separated by at least one container and that they are not stacked on top of each other or in vertical closed dispensers. Make sure the multi-mixer has a dedicated allergen labeled spindle if more than one spindle is available. Ensure that they Vitamix blender has a dedicated allergen labeled jug.    

 

Check for contamination of allergens with another allergen flavor.  Friendly’s now uses brownies from Ghirardelli, which are not considered an allergen item.

 

Exception:  If a mutimixer is present with only one spindle, coach, but do not cite if it is not observed clean and sanitized before being used for guest allergen alert order.

 

 

Picklist:

·        All ice cream allergen flavors do not have red label

·        All ice cream allergen flavors are not stored in a manner that separates them from non-allergen flavors as much as possible in ice cream cabinet

·        All allergen toppings do not have red label/spoon

·        All allergen toppings are not separated or stored below non allergen toppings

·        M&M’s & Reese’s are not separated by at least one container or are stacked on top of each other      

·        Vitamix blender does not have dedicated Allergen labeled jug.      

·        Multimixer does not have dedicated Allergen labeled spindle if more than one spindle
